Terrible-Camera9236 5

The learning curve is brutal but you're actually doing fine - breaking things and fixing them is literally how everyone learns Arch. Two updates breaking different things isn't unusual at all, especially when you're still figuring out the ecosystem

For the sound issue, check if your audio driver got updated and needs a reboot, or if pulseaudio/pipewire services are running. Sometimes the kernel updates mess with audio modules and you just need to reload them. The YouTube thing might be codec related or browser sandbox issues

Steam Deck works because Valve has a whole team maintaining their specific Arch setup and they control exactly what hardware it runs on - way different from us fumbling around on random hardware configs. Don't let that fool you into thinking Arch should be that stable out of the box

Keep that Debian install around as backup while you mess with Cachy, that's smart. The "breakage" fear is overblown anyway - most of the time it's just spending 20 minutes googling error messages and running a few commands
