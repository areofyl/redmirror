It looks like your shared libraries got out of sync, likely during that Flatpak tweak. The manual fix is to try sudo pacman -Syu libxml2 to restore that specific link.

I actually had similar shared library not found errors. I wrote a small Zsh tool called Mend. It identifies which package owns the missing .so file and offers to install it for you so you don't have to hunt for it. Might be worth a look once you're back at your desktop.
GitHub: https://github.com/Rakosn1cek/mend

Better to have it and not needed than the opposite.
